About Us

Your Compliance Journey, Simplified

Pradeep Patil Consultancy is one of India's foremost specialist firms for Bureau of Indian Standards certification. We combine deep regulatory knowledge with a client-first approach to navigate India's most demanding compliance landscape.

From initial assessment to ISI mark approval, our team stands with you at every stage — eliminating uncertainty and helping you bring compliant products to market faster.

We have successfully guided startups, SMEs, and large manufacturers through the BIS certification framework across electronics, chemicals, construction materials, and consumer goods.
